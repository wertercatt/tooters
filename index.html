Tooters.org is a general purpose Mastodon instance for English speakers operated by @victor. All morally grounded individuals capable of civil discourse will feel welcome.

You can submit a request for an account, and review the community rules here.

Moderation

What truly defines a good experience on the fediverse is the attitude taken by your instance operator towards moderating fairly, and ensuring that everyone in the community can have a voice in forming the ruleset. Having been the target of online harassment I have a personally invested interest in keeping this a safe space for everyone.

Bot Accounts

Bots are allowed so long as they are ethical. For Tooters.org that means:

  • ‘This is a bot account’ is toggled on in the account’s profile settings.
  • No more than 100 toots per 24 hour period.
  • No scraping, indexing, reminder, or auto-responders.
  • No unsolicited @ mentioning of any other account.
  • No spam, or advertising.

NSFW Content

Profile avatars may not be sexually explicit, or contain any imagery which would not be safe for work.

Sexually explicit material should have a content warning applied to mark it as sensitive so that the local timeline stays safe for work.

Direct Messaging

Be aware that toots sent to ‘mentioned people only’ (DMs) are not encrypted, and therefore should not be considered truly private.

Account Retention

Should I decide I no longer wish to continue participating in the fediverse, or operating the server then I will give six months advanced notice to account holders so that they can migrate to a different instance.

Server Rules

  • 1. No racism.
  • 2. No sexism.
  • 3. No xenophobia.
  • 4. No transphobia.
  • 5. No homophobia.
  • 6. No misgendering.
  • 7. No antisemitism, Nazism, et Al.
  • 8. No harassment, doxxing, or spamming.
  • 9. No incitement of violence, linking to sources that do, or promoting any such related ideology.
  • 10. All toots in English. Other languages could cause misunderstandings in translation, and would be difficult to accurately moderate.
  • 11. Sexually explicit material should have a content warning applied to mark it as sensitive so that the local timeline stays safe for work.
  • 12. No distribution of disinformation, or otherwise misleading information.
  • 13. No CSAM material, or media/links which violate the DMCA.
  • 14. All media toots should have alt text included because being considerate of accessibility needs is the only way to be a welcoming community.
  • 15. All multi-word hashtags should be capitalized so they are accessible to screen reader technology. As one example: #streetphotography is not readable, but #StreetPhotography is.
